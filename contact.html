FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the LIFTS team?

LIFTS is open to all UPRM students regardless of major. Visit our Careers page to see open positions and submit an application. We recruit at the beginning of each semester.

Can non-UPRM students or community members participate?

While team membership is limited to UPRM students, we welcome community members at our public launch events and educational outreach programs. Follow our social media for event announcements.

How can my organization sponsor a mission?

We offer sponsorship packages at various levels. Contact us at liftsuprm@gmail.com with the subject "Sponsorship Inquiry" and we'll send you our sponsorship prospectus.

Can I fly my experiment on a LIFTS mission?

Yes! We allocate payload space for external experiments on select missions. Reach out with details about your experiment including mass, dimensions, power requirements, and data needs.

Where can I watch a LIFTS launch?

Launch locations vary based on mission requirements and weather. Public launch viewing information is announced on our social media channels when available. Some missions may be live-streamed on YouTube.

How do I request LIFTS for an educational presentation?

We love sharing our work with schools and community groups! Contact us with the subject "Educational Outreach" including your organization, audience age group, and preferred dates.
