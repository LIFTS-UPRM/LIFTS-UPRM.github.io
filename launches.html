Our Journey

Launch Timeline

January 2024

LIFTS Team Founded

A group of UPRM students forms LIFTS and begins preparations for the NEXO mission as part of the Nationwide Eclipse Ballooning Project.

April 8, 2024

NEXO Launch

Successful first launch from Smilin V Scout Ranch in Austin, Texas during the total solar eclipse. Payload reached 95,000 ft and was recovered intact on private property near Tyler, TX (~200 miles from launch).

September 2024

Official Establishment

LIFTS officially established as Launch Initiatives for Technologies in Space, expanding scope beyond eclipse missions.

2025

ASCENT Development

Development of ASCENT mission begins, featuring advanced payloads and expanded scientific objectives.

March 2026

ASCENT Launch

Planned launch of ASCENT mission from Mayagüez, Puerto Rico with multiple scientific payloads.

2028

CubeSat Launch

Target date for UPRM's first student-built CubeSat to reach low Earth orbit.
