Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 January 2026

Summary

We respect your privacy and work to protect the personal information you share with us. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your rights.

Information we collect

Personal information you provide

When you contact us, sign up for updates, apply, or otherwise interact with the site, you may provide information such as your name, email address, organization, and any message you include.

Automatically collected data

We collect usage information (pages visited, referring sources, device and browser data, IP address, and similar analytics) to help improve the site and services.

How we use your information

  • To respond to requests and provide services you ask for.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • To send occasional updates, newsletters, or notifications when you opt in.

Legal basis for processing (where required)

If you are in the EU/EEA, we rely on the legal bases required by data protection law, including your consent, performance of a contract, and our legitimate interests (e.g., analytics and site security).

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, and to improve your experience. You can manage cookies via your browser settings; blocking cookies may affect site features.

Third-party services

We may use third-party services (such as analytics providers or social platforms) that have their own privacy policies. We do not control those services and encourage you to review their policies.

Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection to processing, and data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us as described below.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

For questions about this policy or to exercise your rights, please use our Contact page or email us at privacy@lifts.example.
