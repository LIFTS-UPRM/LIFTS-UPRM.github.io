How to Apply

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Click "Apply Now" on any open position and fill out the application form with your background, interests, and availability.

2

Team Review

Our team leads review applications within 1-2 weeks. We look for passion, dedication, and willingness to learn.

3

Interview

Selected candidates are invited for a casual interview to discuss interests, skills, and how you can contribute to LIFTS.

4

Join the Team

Accepted members join their department, receive onboarding, and start contributing to active missions immediately.
