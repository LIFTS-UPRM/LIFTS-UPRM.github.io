Our Contributors

Building the future of space exploration together

LIFTS would not be possible without the generous support of our partners and contributors. From funding and technical expertise to facilities and mentorship, these organizations enable our student-led team to push the boundaries of near-space research and inspire the next generation of aerospace engineers.

Primary Support

Founding Partners

Organizations that made LIFTS possible from day one

NASA Puerto Rico Space Grant Consortium

Primary funding partner providing critical financial support and connecting us to the broader NASA space grant community and research opportunities.

Collins Aerospace

Industry partner providing technical expertise, hardware support, and mentorship for our high-altitude balloon missions and aerospace systems development.

Home Institution

Academic Partner

University of Puerto Rico, Mayagüez

Our home institution providing facilities, faculty mentorship through the Department of Mechanical Engineering, and administrative support. UPRM's commitment to student research excellence makes our work possible.

Partnerships

Research Collaborations

Organizations we've worked with on specific missions

Nationwide Eclipse Ballooning Project

Multi-university collaboration for the 2024 total solar eclipse. We joined 50+ teams across the U.S. to launch high-altitude balloons during totality, contributing to a nationwide scientific dataset.

Montana State University

Lead institution for the Nationwide Eclipse Ballooning Project, providing technical support, payload design guidance, and coordination for the NEXO mission.
